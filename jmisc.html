Miscellaneous Joy matters

This page contains pointers to several miniature implementations of Joy. None of them are compatible with the current version of Joy, but all of them capture its essence. Maybe they will be of use to some people who want to write up to date versions of Joy in some language other than C.

  • PicoJoy(Prolog) A tiniest Joy-in-Prolog.
  • MiniJoy(Clisp) A small Joy-in-Common Lisp.
  • Joy in Pascal A rather antique exposition of Joy with Pascal source. You also need the scanner from an earlier chapter. Both chapters are from a collection Symbolic Processing in Pascal
