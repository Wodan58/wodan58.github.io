The joy-funs-doc files are containing a brief description of all Joy Atoms and of the Definitions of the Basic Joy Libraries.

The pdf, ps and calltips(1) versions are identical in content. The html version is similar but somewhat smaller and older. Anyhow, it covers all important words of Joy. Pdf and ps have an index, html has not. Please use your browsers search button whenever you are looking for a special word.
In case you don't see anything after downloading the pdf version: scroll down.
